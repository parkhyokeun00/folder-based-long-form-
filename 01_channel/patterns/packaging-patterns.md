# Packaging Patterns

This page stores reusable packaging rules for thumbnails, titles, and upload-facing promise design.

## Thumbnail rules

- prefer clash over explanation
- prefer 2 to 6 Korean words
- the best packaging often sounds like a complaint, shock, or contradiction

## Title rules

- the title should complement the thumbnail instead of duplicating it
- default move: thumbnail = emotional clash, title = explanatory curiosity

## Promise alignment

- the thumbnail, first image, and first 30 seconds must all point at the same promise
- if they point at different promises, retention collapses early

## Warning

- do not push stereotype packaging farther than the script can responsibly support
- controversial cultural topics need a strong hook, but still need a clear fairness line inside the script
